## Speaker Notes

### Home

Hook: "We already wrote the test cases - they are just hidden in code."

One sentence: repo had tests, Xray did not. Our goal: extract, structure, import.

### Problem - VHONANI

Emphasize the why: traceability, reporting, audit. Not AI for AI's sake.

The repo already had the knowledge - we just needed it represented in Xray.

### Approach

AI is a translator, not an executor.

It restructures existing intent. Human validation is mandatory.

### Demo - VHONANI

Clarify: This browser demo is a simplified translator.

The real story: Copilot read real code and produced a CSV mapping required by Xray importer.

### Impact

Use language the broader team cares about: traceability, reporting, audit.

Mention this approach is especially useful when Xray is the source of truth for coverage.

### Guardrails

Say this line: "AI accelerates thinking - responsibility stays with us."

It increases credibility.

### Close

Close confidently: highlight alignment, traceability, and reducing documentation debt.

Invite Q&A about scaling, reliability, and governance.
